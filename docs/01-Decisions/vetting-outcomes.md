# VETTING OUTCOMES — REJECTION IS NOT SILENT

Status: locked

Source: Session Log — 8 July 2026 (Decision #151 amended)

Supersedes the prior "silent rejection" rule.

On a negative vetting outcome an SMS fires to the supplier: *"Approval needs further clarification; please contact admin."* No specific reason is disclosed in the SMS itself.

Reasoning: silence risked supplier frustration, repeated contact attempts, and negative word-of-mouth among therapists. Admin can have the real conversation directly (bad photo, missing qualification), giving her a path to correct and reapply.

**Investigate** outcome is unchanged: no SMS to the supplier, admin-only, loops back into vetting for a second pass.

M1 shows a 3-way outcome after Submit — Approve / Investigate / Reject-equivalent — replacing the single pass/fail. Approve fires the T3-equivalent "pay now" SMS.
